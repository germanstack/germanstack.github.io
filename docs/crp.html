Document Databases && NoSQL

In document databases each key pair with a complex data structure known as a document. Documents can contain many different key-value pairs, or key-array pairs, or even nested documents.

Document databases which is one of the types of NoSQL databases; Graph Stores, Key-Value, and Wide-Columns are also types of NoSQL Databases. NoSQL Databases was developed in response to the demands presented in building modern applications, encompassing a wide variety of different database technologies.

Relational databases were not designed with modern applications in mind; taking advantage of the commodity storage and processing power available today.
